YTT Module 03 · Yoga Darshana

The Intellectual & Spiritual Foundations

Chitta Vritti Nirodha — the cessation of the fluctuations of the mind. This is yoga, as defined by Patanjali.

About This Module

Yoga philosophy is the intellectual and spiritual architecture underlying all yogic practice. At its core is Patanjali's Yoga Sutras — 196 concise aphorisms that map the entire journey from restless mind to absolute stillness.

This module explores the Ashtanga (eight-limbed) framework of Patanjali, key concepts from Samkhya philosophy (purusha and prakriti), the wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita, and the nature of the three gunas — tamas, rajas, and sattva.

We study the yamas and niyamas not as abstract ethics but as living practices that transform the quality of every interaction, every breath, and every moment on and off the mat.
